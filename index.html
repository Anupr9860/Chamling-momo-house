    About Us
    Chamling Momo House

    Welcome to Chamling Momo House, where every momo tells a story of tradition, flavor, and family. Founded with a passion for authentic Nepali cuisine, our journey began not just to serve food, but to share a piece of our culture with the world.

    We specialize in crafting a wide variety of momos, from the classic steamed and fried options to unique, flavorful creations you won't find anywhere else. Each momo is a testament to our commitment to quality, made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our menu also features a selection of refreshing drinks, perfectly paired to complement your meal.

    What started as a single dream has now grown into a thriving family with 10 branches across Nepal. This expansion is a reflection of the love and support we've received from our community, and it drives us to continue our mission: to bring the authentic taste of homemade momos to every corner of our beautiful country.

    Thank you for being a part of our story. We invite you to visit any of our locations and experience a taste of home at Chamling Momo House.
